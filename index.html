About Us

Mission and Vision

Mission:The mission of the school is to provide comprehensive religious and materialistic education to the local community and neighboring areas, emphasizing the development of individuals who are well-grounded in Islamic spirit and culture. The school strives to create a conducive learning atmosphere, free from city hazards and noise pollution.

Vision: Najmul Huda Higher Secondary School aspires to cultivate a community that embodies both cultural and Islamic values, fostering an environment of education, tolerance, and excellence.

History

Established in 1998 under the leadership of Janab Sayyid Umar Ali Shihab Thangal, the Najmul Huda Higher Secondary School is a product of the Najmul Huda Islamic Complex Committee. Located in Kavathikalam, 3.5 kilometers away from Kottakkal, the school was inaugurated with the goal of offering education in a serene and pollution-free environment. From its early days, the school has been dedicated to imparting Islamic values through Madrassa classes conducted under the Samastha Kerala Islam Matha Vidhyabyasa Board.
